Luxurious living in a golfing paradise
There are a variety of living experiences to suit every lifestyle and want in this beautiful enclave of 294 luxury homes, ranging from three-bedroom townhouses to six-bedroom mansions.

Each home is distinguished by contemporary architecture that maximizes natural light, with floor-to-ceiling windows, skylights, and double-height areas all working together to produce a sense of lightness and calm.

Nature-inspired accents, such as weaving trees and flora into the architecture of your house, are paired with a variety of smart home systems that improve your security and convenience while also helping to conserve the environment.
Green Serene Living
Every home in Jouri Hills Arada has direct access to greenery, providing a serene environment for residents to enjoy. The incorporation of green spaces into the development adds to the overall livability and value of the community.
Safety & Security
Our advanced security systems ensure 100% safety with video surveillance and contactless electronic passes.
Transport accessibility

Our development offers easy access to major highways and public transportation, providing residents with convenient and efficient transportation options.

Range of Home Sizes and Floor Plans
We offer a diverse range of home sizes and floor plans to fit every lifestyle, including cozy studios and spacious family homes, all designed for comfort, convenience, and functionality.
Free Golf Cart
Every home purchase includes a free golf cart for your convenience and enjoyment.
